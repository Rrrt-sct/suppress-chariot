Account deletion – ChariotExpress

Users can request the deletion of their account and all associated data.

How to request account deletion

Send an email to: cozzyrocket@gmail.com
Subject: Account deletion request

What happens after the request

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 days.

Deleted data includes

  • User account
  • Shopping lists
  • Personal data associated with the account

Data retention

No data is retained after deletion.
